The Best Movies According to Sid

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Office Space

Sarcastic comedy of life in Silicon Valley. Truths of the culture mixed in with a whacko plot. Brilliantly directed. Cult classic!!

Idiocracy

Parody of where American and western life is headed. Hilarious.

Under Siege

All time greatest martial arts movie after "Enter the Dragon". Brilliantly-stupid 3-line dialogs made for Gen-X types.
